Knight

An all-around unit which does not deal a whole lot of damage and has high upkeep, but most importantly, they hang around as long as they can to protect other units that are behind them. They are the meatshield in case the first row is destroyed. Knight is very strong against Cannon Tower, slightly weak against Hoplite and Helepolis.
