Cavalry

This unit is very strong against Bowmen and Archers, as they can slice into second row in the very beginning; however, they are very fragile relatively to other unit. Their lack of armor is hard to justify without looking into their speed. Cavalry is the fastest unit and provides a speed boost to the overall troop.
