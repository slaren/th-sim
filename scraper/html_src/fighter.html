Fighter

Fighter is a basic unit that can do average damage to most other units. On top of that, fighter health is high enough for them to hold off much damage and protect the weaker units behind them. Fighter is one of the few units that is good against Cannon Tower and Catapult but can fall to bow units if they receive no help. Fighter also carries average amount of loot from the battle which makes up for their lack of damage.
